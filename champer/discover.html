Welcome to City of Capetown

Care for a glass of cape wine? Capetown is one of the largest suppliers of wine in the world.Enjoy the scenery with a glass of wine in your hand .You have the advantage of seeing the grap fields and the processing of the world`s Best wines

Capetown has a very rich history ,some of it is engraved and showcased throught designed and infrasture. The country is called rainbow country for a reason, a place where people of all color and creed can live in harmony and excel in their different lives.

As the saying goes breakfast is the best meal of the day ,fency a breakfast with a glaze of the world famous mountain?. Enjoy your time at V&A waterfront a well established market place that offers you shopping,food,culture etc.

Capetown offers some of the best waters in the world,we have the meeting of two water bodies the atlantice ocean and the indian ocean.You can also find kites for a right and enjoy the refreshing waters.

Water cycling is a faily new enjoyable hobby ,just like a road cycle water cycling is refreshing and fuN.Bring friends and family and race your way to the sunset.

Night life is as beautifull as they come,Lions head hill gives you the best view of Capetown at night .
